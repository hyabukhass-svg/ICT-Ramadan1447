Parking Guidelines

Please carefully review and follow all parking regulations to ensure safety and respect for our community and neighbors.

  • Adhere to I.C.T. Parking Staff instructions at all times.
  • DO NOT block exits or entrances of neighboring properties.
  • DO NOT park behind other vehicles.
  • Pay attention to orange cones and reflectors — they are placed for your safety and others.
  • DO NOT speed. Be mindful of all pedestrians.
  • DO NOT park on the street.
  • Park only in designated parking spaces.
  • DO NOT PARK in neighboring parking lots.
  • DO NOT PARK in tow-away zones. Violators will be towed at owner's expense.
  • Overnight parking is NOT allowed without prior approval from ICT Administration.
Any illegal parking (parking contrary to posted signs and/or contrary to local and city ordinances, policies, and procedures) will be subject to towing at the owner's expense without notice.
We have security protocols in place. Your safety is very important to us. Please be respectful to our traffic and security team and follow their directions.
